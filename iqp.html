Geodome Maker Space

Interactive Qualifying Project at WPI

Project Goal

To design and build a geodome maker space and develop associated curriculum for youth, including those who are at-risk. Students will need to identify developmentally appropriate tools and safety guidelines as well as to develop a curriculum to teach hands-on STEM learning in the maker space.

My Contributon

  • Designed curriculum handbook, which includes robotics, electronic, 3D printing, and art projects, to facilitate STEAM learning for 8 to 12 years old.
  • Developed a CAD model for the maker space to visualize the structure of the geodome to assist the building process.
  • Drafted a research paper to promote nature-based STEM learning for youth, especially for those who are at-risk.
